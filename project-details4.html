Website development as a part of Internship!

  • PCCOER COMPITRON is a chat-bot that is currently active on Google Assistant on Android devices across India.
  • This chat-bot is designed for students of "Pimpri Chinchwad College of Engineering and Research" who have been newly admitted for the First-Year Engineering course.
  • It helps freshers to know about the college including details like teachers, classrooms, subjects, facilities in the college, labs, placements, events conducted, departments, etc.
  • This chat-bot has been approved by "Actions on Google(AoG)" team and is available for all users in India to use.
